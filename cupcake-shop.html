Cupcake Shop Cupcake Slop

Small cupcake shop opens it's doors with big style!

Cupcake Slop Cupcake Shop opened it's doors recently in Nadi, Fiji, breaking the mold and bringing the nation it's first miniature cake culture. Now tourists and native peoples alike can eat cake without a worry of eating too large a portion, because cupcakes are pre-portioned. This brings an end to years of psychological suffering as consumer's in fiji wondered, "what IS the appropriate size for a peice of cake?"
